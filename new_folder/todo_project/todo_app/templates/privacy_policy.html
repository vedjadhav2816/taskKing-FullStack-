Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2025 | Last Updated: March 21, 2025

Welcome to TaskKing! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how TaskKing ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use our website, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). By using TaskKing, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Services. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal Information: When you register an account, use the support form, or upgrade to premium, we may collect:
    • Name
    • Email address
    • Username
    • Payment details (processed via third-party payment providers)
    • Any information you voluntarily provide (e.g., in the support form or profile settings)
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected when you use TaskKing:
    • IP address
    • Device information (e.g., type, operating system)
    • Browser type
    • Pages visited, time spent, and interactions within the app
    • Task-related data (e.g., task titles, due dates, priorities)
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering your login state or preferences. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Create and manage your account, display your tasks, and enable features like profile editing and theme changes for premium users.
  • To Improve TaskKing: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality and user experience.
  • To Communicate: Send account-related updates, respond to support inquiries, or notify you of changes to our Services or policies.
  • To Process Payments: Facilitate premium subscriptions through secure third-party payment processors.
  • To Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, or abuse of our Services.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: Third parties (e.g., hosting providers, payment processors, email services) process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With Affiliates: If TaskKing is part of a larger organization, we may share data with affiliates who adhere to this policy.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety (or that of others).
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred, but we’ll notify you beforehand.

4. Your Choices and Rights

You have control over your information:

  • Access and Update: View or edit your profile details via the sidebar.
  • Delete: Contact us to delete your account and associated data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from non-essential emails using the link in our communications.
  • Cookies: Adjust your browser settings to block cookies, though this may limit functionality.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (e.g., under GDPR or CCPA):

  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Request correction or erasure of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Data portability.

To exercise these rights, email us at taskKing81@gmail.com
